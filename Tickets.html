Pricing

The festival offers affordable ticket options to accommodate different visitors. General tickets provide access to all main events and performances. VIP tickets include premium seating and exclusive experiences. Early booking is encouraged to secure a spot at the event.

Tickets can be purchased online through the registration form below. Visitors are advised to book early to avoid disappointment. Group bookings are also available for families and organizations. This ensures that everyone has an opportunity to attend the festival.

  • General: M150
  • VIP: M300
  • Students: M80
