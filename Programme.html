Event Schedule

The festival programme is carefully planned to provide a variety of activities for all visitors. Each day includes performances, exhibitions, and interactive experiences. Guests can enjoy live music, traditional dances, and food markets. The schedule ensures that there is always something exciting happening.

Visitors are encouraged to explore different activities throughout the festival because they can not afford to miss the opportunity of experiencing that. Workshops and cultural displays offer learning opportunities for tourists. Evening performances create a lively and entertaining atmosphere. The programme is designed to keep guests engaged from start to finish.
